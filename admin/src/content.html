Political Parties in India

BJP (Bharatiya Janata Party)

The Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) is one of the two major political parties in India, along with the Indian National Congress. The BJP is a right-wing party, and its policies generally reflect Hindu nationalist positions.

Congress (Indian National Congress)

The Indian National Congress (INC) is one of the two major political parties in India, along with the Bharatiya Janata Party. The Congress is a center-left party, and its policies generally reflect secular and liberal positions.
